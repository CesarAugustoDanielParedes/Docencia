A Sanctuary for All

Founded on the belief that a quiet moment with a cat can mend even the most hectic day, Purr & Pour was born as a boutique haven in the heart of the city.

Our mission is dual-purpose: to provide a loving, foster-style environment for our resident felines while offering humans a tranquil space to recharge.
